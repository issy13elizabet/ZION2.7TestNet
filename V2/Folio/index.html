Working Story

1

Yosef Hubálek, residing at Jamné nad Orlicí in the Czech Republic, is an individual with a broad range of skills and experiences at an intermediate level. His technical expertise includes web development with a focus on JavaScript, HTML5, CSS, and UX/UI design. In addition, he has significant experience in electrical engineering, woodworking, and construction. Yosef is also an avid adventurer and nature enthusiast, reflected in his interests such as surfing, snowboarding § sk8ing, yachting, and adventure traveling.

2

Yosef's career began in January 2006 at Jioner Fiat s.r.o in Jablonné nad Orlicí, where he worked until January 2008. He then worked as a PC e-shop administrator for Shot Board Shop s.r.o from January 2008 to January 2010, gaining experience in managing online stores. Subsequently, he worked as a carpenter at Haniš s.r.o in Hradec Králové from January 2010 to January 2012, allowing him to refine his woodworking skills.

3

From January 2012 to January 2014, Yosef worked as a fitter for Solar System a.s in Prague, gaining valuable experience in the installation and maintenance of solar systems. His career path then led him to SCHULTE s.r.o, where he worked from January 2014 to January 2016. From January 2016 to January 2024, he engaged in freelance work (OSVC), undertaking various construction projects, including building pergolas and gazebos, and performing electrical engineering tasks.

4

In 2024, Yosef decided to expand his expertise into IT and enrolled in courses for junior web developers and UX/UI design. He completed courses at "It Steep" in August 2024 and "Engeto" from January 2024 to June 2024, where he developed his skills in frontend development. His passion for continuous learning and adapting to new challenges is evident in his career trajectory and personal interests.

5

Outside of his career, Yosef enjoys drawing, an activity that complements his UX/UI design skills. He is speak basic in English and little Portuguese, which enhances his ability to work in diverse environments and fuels his love for traveling and exploring new cultures. His adventurous spirit is evident in his hobbies, such as surfing, snowboarding, and yachting, which not only provide excitement but also inspiration for his creative projects.

6

Overall, Yosef Hubálek exemplifies a versatile and dedicated individual with a wide range of skills and experiences at an intermediate level. His extensive experience across various fields, coupled with his continuous pursuit of new knowledge and skills, makes him a dynamic and multifaceted person. Whether in web development, construction, or adventure sports, Yosef brings a unique blend of technical expertise, creativity, and passion to everything he undertakes.
